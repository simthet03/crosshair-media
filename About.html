About Us

Get to know Crosshair Media and South Africa's student youth market like never before.

Who We Are

Ask yourself this question, how much do you really know about South Africa’s student youth market?

At Crosshair Media, we’ve made it our mission to get to know these young game changers, and we would be more than happy to make an introduction.

As a leader in the influencer marketing category, we have built our reputation on creating relatable connections between brands and students. We provide unparalleled access to this youth market segment, offering relevant and authentic engagement to build your brand with meaningful stories.

Our Story

We started as a niche business in Johannesburg, South Africa, and soon realized we had a rare relationship with this unique, diverse, and active community of empowered individuals.

By nurturing this relationship, we expanded our reach throughout Mzansi and evolved with the market, ensuring we always meet its specific needs.

This partnership gives us one-of-a-kind access to surveys, focus groups, and analytics, providing insights that ensure optimal collaboration for your brand.

Let’s Build Something Meaningful

Speak to us if you’d like to originate highly targeted offers that drive awareness, consideration, acquisition, and ultimately brand advocacy. As an invested partner, we’ll be there to spark innovation and explore better product experiences with our student base.
