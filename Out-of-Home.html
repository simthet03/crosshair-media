Out of Home (OOH)

Innovative strategies for impactful brand visibility.

Overview

In South Africa, Out of Home continues to demonstrate its capacity to reliably reach a specified audience and has proven to be fantastically adaptive and resilient when it comes to the changes brought about by digital and internet advertising.

At Crosshair Media, we see OOH as a high-impact platform that allows us to position your campaign effectively, ensure visibility, and promote brand recall and affinity.

Our campaigns embrace the evolving media landscape, leveraging innovative approaches like Digital Out of Home (DOOH) and Programmatic Digital Out of Home (PDOOH). These strategies ensure your brand reaches the right audience, including South Africa’s progressive and captive student youth market.

Client Spotlight

KFC: Crunch Time

Crosshair Media’s long-standing client, KFC, has consistently leveraged our marquee OOH billboard in Braamfontein to bring bold, high-impact campaigns to life. Over the years, KFC has activated several standout campaigns on this site, including KFC Crunch Master, KFC All Star Legends, and KFC Crunch Time, among many others

These campaigns demonstrate the power of OOH advertising to connect with a young, energetic urban audience. Boosting visibility and reinforcing brand affinity in one of Johannesburg’s most dynamic cultural and commuter hubs.
