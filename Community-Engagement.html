Community Engagement

Empowering brands to connect authentically with South Africa’s youth market.

Why Community Engagement Matters

At Crosshair Media, we recognize that the student youth market are the real experts when it comes to content, and we respect that. Engaging with a community of young, savvy content creators by focusing on user-generated content as the key driver of our social campaigns just makes sense.

We know this audience responds well to visual content, technology, interactive messaging, gamification, experiential marketing, authenticity, and a clear value statement. They are distrustful of what they encounter in the media and certainly know what they want.

When we do community engagement, we curate content to fit niche student personas, aligning this with your campaign and augmenting it with photos, videos, surveys, polls, and independent opinion panels.

Key Engagement Strategies

Visual Content & Technology

Captivate the youth audience with photos, videos, and interactive media.

Interactive Messaging & Gamification

Boost engagement through surveys, polls, and gamified elements.

Experiential Marketing

Deliver authentic experiences that resonate with young individuals.

Clear Value Proposition

Ensure your campaigns present transparent and compelling value.

Platforms We Leverage

A combination of select digital platforms is used, depending on the personas identified. These include TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. We also recommend direct marketing via SMS, WhatsApp automation bots, and email campaigns.
